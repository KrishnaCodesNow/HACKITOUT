**ServiceHub** is a hyperlocal web application built during a hackathon to help people quickly find and book trusted service professionals within their own locality. It focuses on everyday and emergency services such as electricians, plumbers, painters, housekeeping, tutors, mechanics, catering, and general repairs.

The platform prioritizes nearby availability, faster response times, and user safety, making it especially useful during urgent situations. Unlike large aggregator platforms, ServiceHub is designed with a **locality-first approach**, connecting users with nearby providers to reduce wait times and improve trust while empowering local workers.

Finding reliable service professionals in a local area is often time-consuming, especially during emergencies. Users struggle with:
* Delayed responses
* Lack of trust

Meanwhile, local workers often lack digital visibility and structured job access. **ServiceHub** addresses these challenges by creating a simple, fast, and transparent local service discovery system.

## ğŸ’¡ Solution Overview

ServiceHub provides a clean and intuitive interface where users can:
1. Discover services available in their immediate area.
2. Filter by category, experience, and ratings.
3. Instantly book professionals.
4. **Emergency Mode:** Highlight urgent needs for quick handling.
5. **Worker Dashboard:** Allows professionals to manage availability, view jobs, and track earnings.
